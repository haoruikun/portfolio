PC Builder

PC builder is made for users to create PC builds and also view build guides created by me and other users.

How it works:

I proved full CRUD functionality for two features: builds and parts. For builds, users can view build guides and in the home page and the builds page. They can also create, update and delete builds with different permissions. When you are creating a PC build, you need to select PC parts (like cpu, gpu, and cooler) from our database too. This introduces the second set of CRUD functionality for parts. For every part category, users can view, create, update, and delete parts with different permission level.

What you can try to do:

  1. Explore home page, build page, and part pages to view PC build guides and PC parts that is in our database.
  2. Go to builder page, it will ask you to log in. You can use admin credentials or create a new account.
    1. Account: admin
    2. Password: admin
  3. When you finish logging in. You can build your PC from the builder page. When you hit select, it will take you to the parts page and you can select parts from there by clicking add button.
  4. Once you hit the add button, the part will be added to the build.
  5. If you donâ€™t find the part you want, you can also add part to our database by clicking the add part to the list button in the parts page.
  6. When you finish your build, you can see your build in your profile page or in the builds page

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavasScript, PHP, Bootstrap, and jQuery.
